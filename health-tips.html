Proven Health Tips That Actually Work

Backed by science · Easy to apply · Life-changing results

Sleep Better

  • No screens 1 hour before bed
  • Keep bedroom cool (18–20°C)
  • Sleep & wake at same time daily
  • Magnesium or chamomile tea helps

Stay Hydrated

  • Drink 3–4 liters per day
  • Add lemon or cucumber for taste
  • Drink before you're thirsty
  • Track with a marked bottle

Reduce Stress

  • 5-minute breathing (4-7-8 method)
  • Journal 3 things you're grateful for
  • Spend 10 min in nature daily
  • Limit social media to 30 min/day

Move More

  • 10,000 steps = gold standard
  • Stand up every 30 minutes
  • Take stairs instead of elevator
  • Dance while cooking!

Boost Immunity

  • Vitamin C: oranges, bell peppers
  • Zinc: nuts, seeds, eggs
  • Get morning sunlight (10–15 min)
  • 7–9 hours sleep every night

Build Habits

  • Start tiny (2-min rule)
  • Habit stack (after coffee → stretch)
  • Track with app or calendar
  • Celebrate small wins daily
